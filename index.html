Biography

I recently graduated from Arizona State University in December 2019 with concurrent degrees in Physics and Astrophysics. As an undergraduate at ASU, I worked with Danny Jacobs on a number of projects related to the Hydrogen Epoch of Reionization Array (HERA). In particular, I focused on research related to the commissioning of the array and exploring cross-correlation with near-infrared intensity mapping experiments.
